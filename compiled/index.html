Stories on Transition

The only constant in life is change. Five teams of journalism students dove into to this concept, exploring how and why transitions take shape across Chicago and the country. What they found was developed into five interactive stories on how labor, culture, sex, housing and drugs are and reshaping identities and bringing communities in to and out of stages of life.
